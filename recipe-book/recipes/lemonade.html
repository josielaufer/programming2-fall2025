Best Lemonade Recipe

Description

I know some of you are going to see this and think, “Do we really need a lemonade recipe?”. Sure lemonade is one of those things that can be thrown together and still taste great. However, having a reliable recipe is still important. This recipe will create lemonade that is the perfect ratio of sweet and tart. It can easily be doubled or tripled–perfect for lemonade stands! You will love this recipe because it is super smooth and evenly sweetened, thanks to simple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups (1.4 L) water divided
  • 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (236 ml) fresh-squeezed lemon juice seeds removed, pulp may remain

Steps

  1. Combine 1 cup (236ml) water and 1 cup sugar in a small saucepan. Place over medium-low heat and stir until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Remove from heat and set aside, allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  3. Add remaining 5 cups (1180ml) water and stir well.
  4. Pour over ice and serve, or transfer to refrigerator and allow to chill several hours before serving.
