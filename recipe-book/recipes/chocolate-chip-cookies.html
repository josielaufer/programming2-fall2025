Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

This is the best chocolate chip cookies recipe ever! Accessible ingredients, no chilling time and great results. It's simple straight forward and worth the cooking time. They are doughy yet still fully cooked, leaving you satisfied everytime. This recipe is a basic that you can rely on time and time again. These are everything a chocolate chip cookie should be. Crispy and chewy. Doughy yet fully baked. Perfectly buttery and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (12 oz)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius).
  3. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  5. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  6. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).
  7. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  8. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  9. Roll 2-3 Tablespoons (depending on how large you like your cookies) of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
  10. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
  11. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
