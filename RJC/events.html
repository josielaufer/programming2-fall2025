Reproductive Justice Club Website

Past Events

In January 2025, Francis W. Parker School hosted the first annual Youth Reproductive Rights Summit in Chicago. The event featured speakers such as Illinois Lieutenant Governor Juliana Stratton and ACLU leader Ameri Klafeta, along with many other experts. Attendees participated in small-group discussions covering medical, legal, and social justice perspectives while connecting with organizations offering resources and opportunities for action. The summit ended with keynote speeches and reflections that inspired participants to continue advocating for reproductive rights.
