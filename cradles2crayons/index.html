Our Mission

The Cradles 2 Crayons Club's mission at Parker is to collaborate with the organization and bring it to Parker. The club was created to get students involved with the organization and since the organization is for kids in need it's important for students at Parker who are more well off than those kids to be aware of what the organization does. Additionally, Cradles to Crayons was brought to Parker to get more recogntion and to get more volunteers for the organization.
